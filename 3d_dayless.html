Retro FPS
Concept

Dayless is a concept for a stylized, retro survival horror FPS. Inspired by liminal space and brutalism, this game explores the desperation and fear of being lone.

It is set in a North American / Nordic suburban town in the retro-futuristic 1980s. The town is known as Woods1.
