A Town Called Woods is about a seemingly ordinary but mysterious town surrounded by forests in the middle of nowhere. From the surface, it looks like just another average, peaceful town, but there is always something lurking in the shadows. Even with traces of human activity, the town seems to be deserted. Overgrown vegetation, rusty metals, corroded walls, and remnants of previous occupants' possession are what have left in this place.
