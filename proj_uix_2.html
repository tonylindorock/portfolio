Project Q9

To unite all everyday guitar players

We use smartphones every day, and one of the reasons why smartphones became something so convenient and powerful is the ecosystem of moblie applications and services. But is there a great app made for amateur guitarists?

There are countless apps for different services and purposes, but what if we can have everything inside a single app made for everyday guitar players?

And so Project Q9 was born...

Role

UI Designer (2020-2021)


This project is a collaboration between 6 university students across the globe.


While being responsible for the user interface, I also helped redirect our focus from making an app of everything to creating an intuitive guitar learning experience.

Goals

1. Making learning and playing the guitar entertaining and accessible for everyone with user-friendly and consistent designs and experience.

2. Bringing everyday guitarists together to a single platform where they can share and interact.

UI Components

Design

Every element is constructed on the pricinple of encouraging users to learn and play the guitar.


The bright background makes the interface welcoming and friendly. The bold orange accent colour represents the childish feeling of learning something new. Rounded corners and circles have a playful and appealing aesthetic.

4 Main Tabs
From left to right: Home, Connect, Learn, Account

The Discover Tab
Users can buy a new guitar or get their hands on a fresh sheet music.

Playing Mode
Sheet music is built into the app so that users can play along and complete with other users.
